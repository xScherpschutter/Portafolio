> PERSONAL_PROFILE

I am a developer focused on designing and building web and mobile applications, with a clear vision: transforming ideas into useful, scalable, and modern digital solutions. I am passionate about creating systems that not only work efficiently but also offer a smooth and intuitive experience for users.

My approach is based on applying solid architectures such as Hexagonal, Vertical Slice Architecture (VSA), and MVC/MVT, always respecting clean code principles and development best practices. This allows me to build robust, maintainable solutions adaptable to project growth.

In addition, I strategically integrate artificial intelligence into my workflow to optimize processes, reduce times and costs, and maximize efficiency in solution development. Although I am not a DevOps expert, I have experience applying basic concepts to improve deployments and testing environments.

When I am not programming, I dedicate myself to enhancing my knowledge and exploring new technologies. I firmly believe in the power of simplicity and creativity to generate products that truly make a difference for users.
